About Us

PT. MEGACHEMICAL INDONESIA

We are one of leading manufacturers and exporters of industrial chemicals and water treatment chemicals such as aqua ammonia / Ammonium hydroxide 25%, liquid polyaluminum chloride (PAC), ferrous base coagulant and other alum base coagulant.

We also produce specialty oil field chemicals for drilling, production & refinery, water injection, completion & well stimulation.
